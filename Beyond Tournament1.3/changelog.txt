[v1.3.0] The Megaphone Overhaul — 2026-07-10
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

• Megaphone Voice System
- **Per-Player Voice Isolation:** The megaphone PA system now assigns each speaker their own dedicated audio channel. Multiple players can now speak through the megaphone simultaneously without any audio cutting, clipping, or interleaving. Previously, all speakers shared the same output channel, causing voices to chop each other out like a broken plug.
- **Speaker Synchronization Fix:** Resolved a long-standing bug where multiple physical speakers placed on a map would gradually drift out of sync over time. The first few seconds sounded fine, but after a minute the speakers would each play audio at different times. All speakers now share a single timing source per player, guaranteeing perfectly synchronized playback across every cabinet regardless of how many are placed.
- **Scalable Speaker Support:** Maps can now support up to 8 concurrent megaphone speakers without performance degradation. Each player's speaker sources are automatically created on first voice packet and cleaned up after 5 seconds of silence to conserve system resources.
- **Hardware Delay Injection (Stutter Fix):** Overhauled the propagation delay pipeline to instantly queue distance-based silence buffers directly into the OpenAL hardware queue instead of drip-feeding them through Python. This completely eliminates the tiny initial stutter when starting a voice broadcast.
- **Broadcast Collapse Prevention:** Fixed a critical desync where momentary internet lag or stream buffering (underrun) would cause the speakers to lose their staggered timings and play simultaneously (collapse). The engine now detects stream starvation and instantly recalculates and reapplies the correct physical offsets.

• Audio & Music System
- **Music Broadcast Improvements:** Implemented a new routing option for the Music Bot that allows broadcasts to be directed to the physical megaphone system. This includes smooth volume ducking (talkover fade) when voice chat is active on the megaphone, and advanced network stream mixing to ensure crystal clear playback.

[v1.2.1] Tracking & Stability Update — 2026-07-09
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

• Accessibility & Spatial Awareness
- **Object Tracking System:** Press T to open the tracking menu and locate any object around you — doors, weapon buys, perk machines, arcade tables, named zones, and other players. Each entry reports the real item name, distance in tiles, and an 8-way compass direction (or "right here" when standing on it). Once tracked, a periodic 3D beacon plays at the object's position with a pitch that rises as you face it and falls as you turn away, letting you home in by ear alone. Hold Alt+T for a quick spoken status of your current target. Zombies, hellhounds, and walls are intentionally excluded.

• Performance & Stability
- **High-Round Ping Spike Fix:** Resolved severe latency spikes (300-400ms+) that appeared around round 9-10 and onward of zombie mode, especially at the start of each new round. The root cause was freshly spawned zombies all attempting to compute navigation paths simultaneously, saturating the server. Pathfinding work is now torn down the instant an entity dies (instead of lingering for up to a second), in-flight searches are cancelled early, and each zombie now staggers its pathfinding on a randomized schedule so they no longer all calculate at once. Rounds now start noticeably smoother.

• Gameplay Balance
- **Helper Companion Limit:** The maximum number of allied AI helpers a player can deploy at once has been reduced from 5 to 3, and the starting helper charges granted when joining a zombie match have been lowered accordingly. This keeps combat readable and reduces late-game load without removing the feature.

[v1.2.0] The Arsenal Update — 2026-07-06
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

• Zombie Mode & Arsenal Expansion
- **Massive Arsenal Upgrade:** The fight against the undead just got significantly more explosive! We have introduced a massive wave of over 40 new military-grade firearms and heavy weapons into the survival armory.
- **Hyper-Realistic Weapon Audio:** The audio engine has been completely overhauled to deliver terrifyingly immersive 3D spatial sounds. Every gunshot, reload, and empty magazine click now echoes dynamically through the environment, bringing a new level of tension to every encounter.

• Audio Engine & Reverb System
- **Voice Chat Memory Stabilization:** Resolved a severe driver-level memory leak where active voice streams and music bots would continuously allocate system audio buffers instead of recycling them. This prevents audio degradation, crackling, and channel collapse after extended play.
- **Environmental Reverb Synchronization:** Fixed a bug where players connecting to a map would hear dry (reverb-free) voice and music playback from stationary entities. The audio engine now dynamically binds the appropriate environmental reverb effects upon spawn.
- **Teardown Stability:** Improved resource cleanup on megaphone speakers and map transitions by ensuring all auxiliary EFX sends are properly detached before recycling hardware audio channels, eliminating the occasional buzzing and mono transition glitches.
- **Radio Chat Crash Fix:** Resolved background crashes in the radio communication channel, restoring stable team voice communication.

[v1.1.0] The Minigames & Competition Update — 2026-07-05
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

• Competitive League System (Ranked Matchmaking)
- **Global MMR & Tier Progression:** Introducing the official Elo-based ranked system! All players begin their journey at 500 MMR (Normal tier) and can climb through 7 prestigious ranks: Normal, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Master, and Grandmaster. Beating a higher-ranked opponent yields massive MMR gains!
- **Skill-Based Matchmaking & Quick Join:** Added the "Queue for Ranked 1v1" option to the Minigame menu. The matchmaking engine scans for opponents within your skill bracket (±200 MMR) to ensure perfectly balanced competition. Additionally, players can now instantly jump into the arena by typing `/battle` or `/accept` in the global chat whenever they hear a Ranked broadcast!
- **Dynamic Speed (Endurance Mode):** To truly test the reflexes of top-tier players, all Ranked matches force "Dynamic Speed" to ON. The ball's starting speed scales with your rank, but every successful hit makes the ball accelerate infinitely! Player walking speed remains locked, forcing you to rely purely on anticipation and reaction time to survive.
- **Adrenaline Match Alerts:** The moment a worthy opponent is found, both the challenger and the waiting host will receive an instant, adrenaline-pumping audio alert and a server-wide broadcast to prepare for battle.
- **High-Elo Spectator Integration:** Spectators browsing the active match list will now see the exact Tier/Rank (e.g., Diamond, Grandmaster) of ongoing Ranked matches. This helps audiences easily find and tune into high-level competitive gameplay to watch the pros battle it out.
- **Official Rules Integration:** The official gameplay rules and regulations for 1v1, 2v2, and Ranked League have been formally updated. Players can view the full rulebook directly in-game via the Pong menu.

• Minigames & Arcade Features
- **Minigame Menu Foundation:** Reorganized the Minigame menu to introduce a game selection layer. Players now first select which minigame to play (e.g., "Pong") before choosing match options, laying the structural groundwork for easily adding multiple new minigames in the future.
- **Pong Statistics Tracking:** Added persistent database tracking for Pong (1v1 Wins, 2v2 Wins, Losses, and Longest Rally).
- **Anti-Farming Protections:** Competitive Pong matches will instantly invalidate and disable stat tracking if a human player leaves and an AI substitutes in, ensuring the leaderboard remains fair.
- **Instant Record Saving:** The Longest Rally record is now saved immediately to the database upon scoring a goal, preventing players from losing their epic rally records if a match is abandoned.
- **Leaderboard Categorization:** The main leaderboard is now cleanly divided into "Zombie Game" and "Minigames" categories, preventing competitive stats from getting cluttered.
- **Arcade Table Power Requirements:** Arcade Tables are now fully integrated into the map's electrical grid.
  - Players must interact with a "Power Switch" to restore power before tables can be used.
  - If a map lacks a Power Switch, the table safely locks itself and instructs the player to contact a builder.
- **Arcade AI Start Mechanics:** Playing against the AI at an Arcade Table no longer auto-starts the match instantly. Players are now prompted to manually start the match via the main menu, giving them time to position themselves.

• Audio & Music System
- **Missing UI Sounds Fix:** Resolved an underlying network parsing error where certain UI sound effects (such as the adrenaline alert when a Ranked Match is found) were silently failing to trigger on the client side.
- **Music Bot Privacy:** The integrated Music Bot now defaults to "Private Listening Mode" upon activation to prevent accidental server-wide broadcasts. Players must explicitly toggle broadcasting (Alt+M) to share their stream.
- **Music Bot Broadcast Constraints:** Added strict rules to prevent audio trolling during Pong matches.
  - Broadcasting is now strictly disabled in Competition and Training arenas. (Players attempting to broadcast will receive an automated voice warning).
  - Arcade matches remain unrestricted, allowing sandbox players to broadcast freely.
  - Spectators in a Competition match can now freely access their personal Music Bot (M, F9, F10) for private listening without interrupting the match.

• Core System & Security
- **Global Anti-Cheat Framework:** Implemented a strict Client-Side anti-tamper system. The game now actively monitors for unauthorized third-party memory modification tools and time-manipulation software (Speedhacks). If cheating software is detected running in the background, the client will terminate immediately to protect competitive integrity. Additionally, the system now features enhanced monitoring that automatically forwards real-time incident reports directly to the moderation team, ensuring all tampering attempts are swiftly reviewed to maintain a fair environment.
- **Thai Keyboard Layout Compatibility:** Fixed an issue where players using the Thai keyboard layout (or other non-English layouts) were unable to trigger hotkeys (such as opening the chat with '/' or moving with 'WASD'). The game engine now seamlessly normalizes keystrokes in the background, allowing players to use all hotkeys normally while still being able to type native characters perfectly in the chat.
- **Server Crash Prevention (Anti-Thundering Herd):** Redesigned the client connection protocol during unexpected server reboots. To prevent sudden CPU spikes and server crashes caused by hundreds of clients simultaneously attempting to auto-reconnect, the game will now safely return players to the main menu upon connection loss. Players must now log in manually, which safely and naturally staggers the network load.
- **Client Log Management:** Fixed an organizational issue where client diagnostic logs and chat buffers would save directly into the root of the user's 'Documents' folder. All exported files are now neatly nested and organized inside a dedicated 'Beyond Tournament' folder.
- **Client Crash Prevention (Buffer Sanitization):** Fixed a critical vulnerability where receiving specific server alerts would cause the client to attempt to create log files with illegal path characters, resulting in an instant game crash. All background logging systems have been rigorously sanitized.
- **Server Message Overhaul (MOTD):** Completely rewrote the Message of the Day to officially introduce and welcome players to the new Ranked League system.

[v1.0.0] The "Beyond Tournament" Overhaul — 2026-07-04
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

• 🚀 The Launch of v1.0
Welcome to version 1.0! This massive update officially rebrands the project to "Beyond Tournament" and introduces critical structural, networking, and security improvements to finalize the core foundation of the game.

• Core System & Security
- **Project Rebrand:** All internal and external references have been officially migrated to the new "Beyond Tournament" branding.
- **Enhanced Connection Security (Silent Drops):** Implemented strict protocol version checking. Connections from outdated clients or unauthorized software are now seamlessly dropped at the protocol layer to protect server stability and prevent packet sniffing.
- **Smart Logging (Client):** Radically improved how the client handles debug logs. 
  - Developer Mode (Running via Scripts): The client will no longer generate a `client_debug.log` file, keeping development directories perfectly clean.
  - Production Mode (Compiled Executable): The client automatically generates a debug log file next to the `.exe` to capture crashes and unhandled exceptions for easier player troubleshooting.
- **Map Isolation & State Protection:** Completely isolated Minigames (Pong) from live map edits. If a builder reloads or edits the host map, players inside Arcade Tables will no longer experience visual stutters or persistent sound loops.
- **Standalone Arena Reload Fix:** Fixed a bug where standalone Pong arenas (created via main menu) were incorrectly isolated from map reloads, preventing hosts from updating music or map objects mid-match.

• Social & Communication
- **Dynamic Channel Language System:** Implemented a massive multi-language Global Chat system supporting over 180 languages worldwide (e.g., Thai, English, Japanese, and many more).
  - **Database Persistence:** The server automatically remembers each player's chat language preference across sessions.
  - **Smart Default:** New players, or those who haven't selected a language, will automatically default to the Thai (th) channel.
  - **Live Player Counts:** The `Ctrl+L` language menu now fetches real-time data from the server, displaying the exact number of players currently active in each language channel (e.g., "Thai 5 players"). Channels with 0 players will simply show the language name.
  - **Accessibility & UX Improvements:** 
    - The menu automatically focuses on the player's current language upon opening.
    - Improved TTS (Text-to-Speech) formatting to clearly announce the current selection without interrupting the menu title (e.g., "Select your channel language... Current Thai 5 players").
  - **Live Server Broadcast Translation:** Integrated a real-time translation engine for the Message of the Day (MOTD) and Global Server Announcements (e.g., Reboot warnings).
    - Uses a lazy-caching mechanism to ensure zero login delay and prevent API rate-limits.
    - Excludes Thai ('th') and English ('en') channels from translation to preserve the original text for the primary player base.
    - Players in foreign language channels will receive all global system messages dynamically translated into their preferred language.

• Gameplay & Audio Updates
- **Reverb & PA Fixes:** Fixed severe OpenAL EFX resource leaks where changing maps frequently caused Megaphone (PA System) audio to corrupt the environmental 3D reverb, turning it into a buzzing mono sound.
