• Audio Engine & Sound Systems
- **UI Sound Preloading:** All critical notification and interface sounds are now pre-loaded into memory at startup, eliminating the issue where alert sounds would fail to play on first trigger.
- **Audio Buffer Retention:** Strengthened the audio buffer cache to prevent premature sound disposal between plays, ensuring consistent playback reliability.
- **Weapon Reload Sound Overlap Fix:** Fixed the reload sound effect playing twice simultaneously. Reload audio now plays once cleanly, matching firing sound behavior.

• Notifications & Alert Sounds
- **Dedicated Broadcast Sound:** Global server announcements now use a distinct notification tone separate from personal messages.
- **Staff Warning Sound Integration:** Administrative warnings, security alerts, and moderation actions now trigger a dedicated warning tone for staff.
- **Kick Alert Broadcast:** Kick and ban events now broadcast a distinct alert sound globally for all connected players.
- **Anti-Cheat Stealth Alerts:** Security detection alerts are now delivered exclusively to staff with a warning sound, while other players see the flagged user go offline normally.

• Megaphone & Broadcasting
- **Broadcast Ownership Lock:** Added server-enforced broadcast ownership to prevent overlapping megaphone transmissions.
