• Weapon Manager & Admin Tools
- **Categorized Weapon Management:** Reorganized the weapon management interface into clean categories (Melee, Semi-Automatic, Full Automatic, All Weapons) for streamlined navigation and administration.
- **Target DPS Equalizer:** Added automated bulk DPS balancing across firearm categories, calculating damage per bullet based on rate of fire while enforcing safety caps and protecting unique equipment.

- **Screen Reader Accessibility Clamps:** Optimized input prompts for screen reader clarity by removing pre-filled default numbers and establishing safety boundaries against extreme stats.

• Builder & Menu Navigation
- **Category-Aware Back Navigation:** Configured weapon inspection options to preserve category origin, returning administrators directly to their active weapon category upon exiting weapon property sub-menus.
- **Step-by-Step ESC Navigation:** Enforced layer-by-layer menu back navigation when pressing the Escape key, eliminating full-stack menu closures and matching builder navigation standards.
- **Arrow-Key Volume Hijack Fix:** Left and right arrow keys were unconditionally hijacked to adjust the preview volume in every menu. The volume handlers are now gated on the sound-browse flag so arrows no longer interfere with normal menus.

• Audio Engine & Voice Chat
- **Megaphone Talkover Clipping Fix:** Replaced ducking with equal-power sum-safe mixing across active speakers, preventing clipping and distortion during simultaneous megaphone usage.
- **Non-Blocking Builder Sound Preview:** Sound previews in the Builder music/ambience browse menus are now decoded on a background thread, eliminating audio stuttering during sound browsing.
- **Zombie Death Audio Refinement:** Removed human collapse impact sounds during zombie elimination, ensuring zombies exclusively play thematic vocal death sounds.
