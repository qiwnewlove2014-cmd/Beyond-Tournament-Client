# 🎉 Beyond Tournament Client - The Awakening Update (v1.0.0)

*(Note: The internal client build version shown in-game is `v0.33.6`, unified under public release `v1.0.0`)*

Welcome to the official public release of the **Beyond Tournament Client**!
We have completely overhauled the client experience, transforming it into a deeply immersive, feature-rich audiogame platform. 

## 🎧 Advanced Audio & Systems
- **Music Bot Privacy Update:** The in-game Music Bot now starts in a private listening mode by default to prevent accidental live broadcasts. Players must intentionally toggle broadcasting (via Alt+M) to share music with the server.
- **Enhanced Connection Security:** We have upgraded the connection protocol to seamlessly drop outdated software or mismatched connections at the core network layer, guaranteeing tighter stability.
- **Major System Maintenance:** Completed a visual and structural overhaul of internal files, separating user configuration paths into a more optimal layout for long-term support.
- **PA Megaphone System:** Experience realistic stadium-style broadcasts. Our engine now simulates dynamic speaker positioning and physical line-of-sight audio occlusion (sound is blocked by solid walls).
- **In-Game Music Bot:** Powered by YouTube integrations (`yt-dlp` & `ffmpeg`), you can now seamlessly stream and listen to your favorite music directly inside the game world together with other players.

## 🎮 Overhauled UX & Mechanics
- **Multi-Account Manager:** The newly designed main menu features a robust account manager, allowing you to easily save, switch, and delete multiple profiles locally.
- **Spectator Mode:** Press TAB to seamlessly switch perspectives. Our audio engine smoothly fades and transitions sounds when spectating other players.
- **Expanded Controls:** Enjoy an expanded hotbar for your weapons and items, alongside dedicated quick-binds for the new Builder and Helper menus.

## 🚀 Rock-Solid Stability
- **Smart Crash Reporter:** We've implemented a brand-new GUI crash reporter. If the shadows prove too much and the game falters, you'll receive a clean, readable dialog box to help us track down the bug immediately.
- **Resource Pooling:** A rewritten OpenAL EFX object pooling system ensures you never run out of audio channels during intense zombie hordes.

Jump in, grab your gear, and survive the Beyond Tournament. 🌒
